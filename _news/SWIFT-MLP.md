Registration is now open for **SWiFT-MLP: Semi-Automated Workflows for Facilitating Multimodal Language Processing**, a hands-on workshop to be held at **MMSYM 2026** in **Leuven, Belgium**, on **8 September 2026**. The workshop will be co-located with the **Symposium Series on Multimodal Communication** (9–11 September 2026).

SWiFT-MLP is designed for researchers and students working with audio and video data who are interested in practical, reproducible workflows for analysing multimodal communication. The workshop focuses on semi-automated pipelines for multimodal language processing, including **body keypoint extraction with MediaPipe**, **gesture segmentation**, **speech transcription with WhisperX**, **exporting annotations to tools such as Praat and ELAN**, and **comparing speech and gesture signals for multimodal and kinematic analysis**.

The programme will be especially relevant to researchers in multimodal communication, linguistics, NLP, computer vision, and cognitive science who need transparent and reusable methods for studying embodied interaction.

Applications are open until **30 April 2026**. A maximum of **20 participants** will be accepted. The registration fee is **100 euros**, which includes training, coffee breaks, lunch, and an evening social event. Official registration and payment will take place after selection, and confirmation emails will be sent by **15 May 2026**.

Workshop website: https://swift-mlp.github.io/2026/
